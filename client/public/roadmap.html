Our Roadmap

At MotoMob, we are committed to enhancing the lives of motorcyclists. To achieve this goal we continually strive to improve our platform based on what our users tell us, what we infer from vast amounts of data collected and our vision for the future.

Our passionate team is committed to creating the best possible experience for motorbikers around the world. Here is the list of improvements and features we will be working on in the next 3-6 months

If you see something broken or you have any questions/suggestions you can write to us at hello@motomob.me. The platform also has a “share feedback” button that you can use to share your thoughts.

Story/Editor

(Oct 2020)

As motorcyclists, we love travelling to amazing places on our bikes. And, we love to share our experiences with our fellow bikers!

With our simple and powerful editor, our users can include photos, video links and organise content easily thus enriching the overall story-telling experience!

Photo Contest

(Nov 2020)

As bikers, we love riding to amazing places on our bikes. We love seeing new cultures, landscapes, people: The world! We also love taking amazing pictures with our motorcycles!

MotoMob photo contest allows our members to share their pictures and win great prizes. Anyone can enter a competition and the most “likes” wins!

The Marketplace

(Dec 2020/Jan 2021)

As bikers, we want a secure, trusted place where we can purchase parts for the motorcycles we ride and gear that matches our style of riding. MotoMob is a secure market place designed to help you purchase parts and gear that matches your need. It takes away the need for extensive research and the anxiety of not knowing if the parts purchased are indeed the right fit.

This is a new way to shop and sell your motorcycles, parts, accessories and gear.

The Mobile App

(Jan 2021)

The current application is primarily designed for the web. Whilst it does work on mobiles, it is less than ideal. Our mobile app will enable you to use MotoMob on the go with a smile. The Mobile up will be using native platform technologies to create a superior experience for riders.
