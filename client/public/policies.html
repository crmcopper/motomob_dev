Member Code of Conduct

As we are a startup building a new platform, we have not composed a detailed set of rules and standards for our members to follow. As we start to grow, we will enhance our community standards. In the meantime, we would like all our members to exercise sound judgement and make everyone feel welcome.

  1. Don't deliberately offend anyone. We know it can be a grey area.
  2. Try not to be offended. Laugh it off.
  3. Be genuine.
  4. Nothing fake.
  5. We are not Facebook or Twitter; MotoMob is about motorcycles, riders and travelling on motorbikes; so please just stick to that.

Whilst we seldom would, in extreme circumstances, we hold the unequivocal right to take steps to protect our platform and members. In such situations, we will be transparent and we will communicate with our members about any decisions we take.
